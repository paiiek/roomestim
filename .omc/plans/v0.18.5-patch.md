### 0.3 Why this is worth a PATCH (not "just noise")

  own pace. These test files are **internal callers**, not external compatibility
  consumers ‚Äî they should already be on `MeshAdapter`. Migrating them is the
  intended end-state of D33's migration path.
- The shim's docstring (`roomestim/adapters/polycam.py:3-4`) reads "Scheduled for
  removal at v0.14 or later (decisions.md D33)". We are at v0.18.4, **past v0.14**,
  and removal never happened. Honest observation only ‚Äî removal stays OUT OF SCOPE
  this PATCH (¬ß2.4 / D33 reverse-criterion). Migrating internal+test callers off
  the alias is a *prerequisite* toward an eventual removal, but this PATCH **removes
  nothing**.

### 1.4 Why the swap is behavior-preserving (verified at HEAD `35e691d`)

- `PolycamAdapter(MeshAdapter)` (`roomestim/adapters/polycam.py:20`). Its `parse()`
  override does exactly two things before delegating to `super().parse()`: (i)
  emits the `DeprecationWarning` (`:38-42`), (ii) routes `.json` ‚Üí
  `RoomPlanAdapter` (`:44-45`). For a `.obj` input, branch (ii) is NOT taken; the
  call falls through to `super().parse(path_obj, scale_anchor=‚Ä¶, octave_band=‚Ä¶)`
  (`:46-48`) ‚Äî i.e. `MeshAdapter.parse` with the identical signature.
- Therefore `MeshAdapter().parse(<obj>, scale_anchor=None, octave_band=False)`
  produces the **same `RoomModel`** as `PolycamAdapter().parse(<same obj>, ‚Ä¶)`,
  minus the warning. **Verified at plan time**:
  `MeshAdapter().parse('tests/fixtures/lab_room.obj', scale_anchor=None,
  octave_band=False)` ‚Üí 6 surfaces, NO warning under `-W error::DeprecationWarning`.
- `MeshAdapter._SUPPORTED_SUFFIXES = frozenset({".obj", ".gltf", ".glb", ".ply"})`
  (`roomestim/adapters/mesh.py:37`) ‚Äî `.obj` is supported; `.json` is not (this is
  why ¬ß2.3 cli.py must stay on `PolycamAdapter`).

## 2. Invariants the fix must NOT break (verified)

### 2.1 The deprecation CONTRACT test ‚Äî MUST stay byte-untouched & GREEN

**Action: DO NOT edit this file.** Verified GREEN at HEAD: `3 passed, 3 warnings`.

### 2.2 The alias export + shim module ‚Äî MUST stay

- `roomestim/adapters/__init__.py:7` (`from roomestim.adapters.polycam import
  PolycamAdapter`) and `:10` (`__all__ = [‚Ä¶ "PolycamAdapter" ‚Ä¶]`) ‚Äî UNCHANGED.
- `roomestim/adapters/polycam.py` (the 49-line shim) ‚Äî UNCHANGED (incl. the stale

### 2.3 TRAP ‚Äî `roomestim/cli.py:303-306` `_get_adapter("polycam")` ‚Äî MUST stay on `PolycamAdapter`

`PolycamAdapter.parse` delegates `.json` ‚Üí `RoomPlanAdapter` (`polycam.py:44-45`).

`roomestim ‚Ä¶ --backend polycam <file>.json`. **Action: DO NOT touch
`roomestim/cli.py`.** (The DeprecationWarning when a user *explicitly* passes
`--backend polycam` is arguably intended feedback that the backend name is legacy.)

### 2.4 Full alias removal ‚Äî stays DEFERRED (D33 reverse-criterion)

D33 (`decisions.md:1243`) reverse-criterion: "If a v0.14 release decides to drop
`PolycamAdapter` entirely (full removal of the alias), that lands under a successor
D-decision and a `RELEASE_NOTES_‚Ä¶` 'Breaking changes' callout." No such successor D
is allocated this cycle; D62 explicitly does NOT remove anything (¬ß1.2). Removal

### 2.5 Runtime source / schema / acoustic / web byte-equality ‚Äî untouched
