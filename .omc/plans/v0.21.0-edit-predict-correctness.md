## Context (verified by reading the code)

**OQ-43 — two coexisting "surface index" frames, no shared authority.**
- `evolve_room_material` (`edit.py:212-221`) and `evolve_room_materials_bulk`
  (`edit.py:252-262`) index into the **full** `room.surfaces` list. This is
  **CORRECT for their only shipping caller**: web `on_apply_overrides`
  (`material_override.py:126`) feeds them indices produced by
  `_dataframe_to_changes_json` (`material_override.py:155-162`), which enumerates
  `room.surfaces` by full-list index (e.g. `{"0": ...}` = the floor). **Do NOT
  change the signature/semantics of these two helpers.** (Contract verified.)
- `Object.wall_index` resolves on the **walls-only** filtered list in two
  consumers, both already correct + ADR 0037-locked:
  predictor `_objects_to_wall_alpha_overrides` (`predictor.py:295-301`, walls list
  built at `:461`/`:535`) and viewer `_wall_subquad` (`viewer.py:252`).
- Adapter surface order is NOT uniform (`roomplan.py` `[floor,*ceilings,*walls]`;
  `mesh.py` `[floor,ceiling,*walls]`; `ace_challenge.py` trailing floor), so the
  walls-only frame is the one with no single resolver — a future "edit wall N"
  feature wiring a walls-relative index into `evolve_room_material` would patch the
  WRONG surface. **Not a current regression** (no shipping caller does this), but
  the exact latent condition that produced the v0.19.0 defect. Fix is **additive +
  defensive only**.

**OQ-44 — three silent behaviors on bad/edited input (all verified):**
- (a) `predict_rt60_default` (`predictor.py:479-491`) and `_per_band`
  (`:555-567`) catch `ValueError/IndexError` from `_objects_to_wall_alpha_overrides`
  and silently downgrade the **whole room** ISM→Eyring; the offending `wall_index`
  (carried in the exception text) is discarded from the rationale.
- (b) `object_add.py` (`_on_add_object:100` → `Object(...)`) accepts any
  `wall_index = int(raw)` with no upper bound; reader `_parse_object`
  (`room_yaml_reader.py:110-111`) and `roomplan.py:169-170` apply no bound either.
- (c) `evolve_surface` (`edit.py:91-93`) **unconditionally** sets
  `absorption_bands = MaterialAbsorptionBands[material]` on a material change,
  promoting a single-band surface (`absorption_bands=None`, the `octave_band=False`
  ingest default) to per-band.

  - Leave `predictor.py:136` (docstring) and `binaural.py:57` (operates on a passed
    `surfaces` list, not a `RoomModel`) **untouched** — out of frame.

  (analogue of `tests/test_wall_index_frame.py`):
  - On a room with order `[floor, ceiling, wall, wall, wall, wall]`

    assert `wall_surfaces(room)[k] is room.surfaces[surface_index_for_wall(room, k)]`.

  - Assert `surface_index_for_wall(room, len(wall_surfaces(room)))` raises `IndexError`.
