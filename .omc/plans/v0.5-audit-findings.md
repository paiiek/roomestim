## Finding 1 (split) — dimensions DONE; materials DEFERRED

### Dimensions — DONE at v0.5.0

The parallel `cwm:websearchwithme` research surfaced **arXiv:1606.03365 Table 1**
(TASLP supporting material; open access; transcribed 2026-05-06) as a
canonical source for per-room L×W×H. All 7 rooms in `ACE_ROOM_GEOMETRY` are
verified against arXiv Table 1 within ±0.01 m by the new gated audit at
`tests/test_ace_geometry_audit.py`, using the committed fixture
`tests/fixtures/ace_eaton_2016_table_i_arxiv.csv`.

One genuine numerical mismatch was found and patched at v0.5.0:

| Room | v0.4 (L, W, H) | v0.5 (L, W, H) | Source |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `Office_2` | (5.10, 3.50, 3.00) | (5.10, 3.22, 2.94) | arXiv 1606.03365 Table 1 |

V_m³ for `Office_2` shrinks 53.55 → 48.28 (~10%); Sabine RT60 prediction at
500 Hz drops correspondingly. The regenerated perf doc at
`docs/perf_verification_e2e_2026-05-07.md` (when corpus is available — see
the §F note in `.omc/plans/v0.5-design.md` and the orchestrator brief)
reflects the new value.

Three rooms (`Office_1`, `Office_2`, `Building_Lobby`) carry an L/W swap
between roomestim's "longer dimension as L" convention and arXiv Table 1's
ordering. Products and V_m³ are identical; the L/W swap on `Office_1` and
`Building_Lobby` is product-equivalent and was NOT patched. ADR 0010 declares
the convention.

### Materials — DEFERRED to v0.6+

Material assignments (`floor`, `walls`, `ceiling`) for the 7 rooms were not
findable in any open-access source: not in the local corpus, not in arXiv
1606.03365, not on the Imperial College SAP project page, not on Zenodo,
and not on author home pages. The materials are presumed to live only in
the Eaton 2016 TASLP final paper (paywalled).

This blocks Finding 3 (Lecture_2 ceiling hypothesis) and the assignment-
correctness pre-condition of D14's 5b reverse-trigger. Both stay DEFERRED.

The honesty caveat in `roomestim/adapters/ace_challenge.py` was rewritten at
v0.5.0 to declare materials NOT cross-checked, and the auto-regenerated
audit doc at `docs/ace_geometry_audit_2026-05-07.md` carries the same caveat
in its header.

## Finding 4a — MaterialAbsorptionBands coefficient revision (DEFERRED)

D14's reverse-trigger 5b requires both "assignments correct" AND "errors
persist on >2 rooms with Eyring after the geometry fix". The first
pre-condition is indeterminable without Finding 1 materials half. F4a stays
DEFERRED.

The dimensional fix at Office_2 marginally reduces the Office_2 row's
over-prediction at 500 Hz (Sabine drops ~10% from V_m³ shrink). The other
high-error rooms (Building_Lobby, Lecture_1, Office_1, Lecture_2) are
unaffected by the dimensional fix.

## Finding 4b — MISC_SOFT enum extension (DONE)

`MaterialLabel.MISC_SOFT = "misc_soft"` is added to the closed enum. The
absorption tables gain one row each:

- `MaterialAbsorption[MISC_SOFT] = 0.40` (legacy 500 Hz scalar).
- `MaterialAbsorptionBands[MISC_SOFT] = (0.20, 0.30, 0.40, 0.50, 0.60, 0.65)`.

The band-2 ↔ legacy-scalar invariant
(`test_band_a500_matches_legacy_scalar`) extends automatically because it
iterates over `MaterialLabel`. Three explicit new MISC_SOFT tests plus a
fourth invariant-reinforcement test bring the default-lane count from 80
to 84. ADR 0011 records the rationale, the alternatives considered, and
the reverse-trigger.

`MISC_SOFT` is a schema slot only at v0.5: no adapter currently emits a
`MISC_SOFT` surface. Adapter wiring follows consumer demand (D5 precedent
for `x_aim_*` extension keys reserved before consumer wiring).

## Status update 2026-05-07 (v0.5.1 — TASLP final reviewed)

The Eaton 2016 TASLP final paper (DOI 10.1109/TASLP.2016.2577502) was obtained
via SNU IEEE Xplore on 2026-05-07. Reviewing it cover-to-cover **invalidates
the v0.5.0 framing of "Materials — DEFERRED to v0.6+ because TASLP-blocked"**.

table. §II-C "Rooms" (p.1683) is one prose sentence per room with **floor type
+ furniture counts only**; walls and ceiling are not specified for any of the

### Building_Lobby structural caveat (NEW)

TASLP §II-C describes Building_Lobby as a "large irregular-shaped hard-floored
room with coupled spaces including a café, stairwell and staircase. Measurements
in Table I correspond to the corner area where the recordings were made whereas
the total volume of the lobby is many times larger." This is a **modelling-
assumption** caveat: `ACE_ROOM_GEOMETRY["Building_Lobby"]` shoebox of 72.9 m³
describes the recording corner, not the room. Sabine RT60 prediction on a
non-shoebox coupled space is not expected to match measurement; v0.4's
+1.425 s err Sabine on Building_Lobby is consistent with this assumption
violation, **not (only)** a coefficient/material gap.

### MISC_SOFT retroactive justification (Finding 4b strengthened)

TASLP §II-C explicit furniture counts (Office_2 "6 chairs + bookcase";
Lecture_2 "~100 chairs + ~35 tables"; etc.) are now the direct empirical
motivation for v0.5.0's MISC_SOFT enum slot. Per-room MISC_SOFT surface area
can be derived from these counts via Beranek 2004 / Vorländer 2020 per-piece
equivalent absorption — **out-of-scope for v0.5.x; available for v0.6+**.
